About

I am a fourth-year student at University of Maryland, College Park where I study Computer Science. I am a software engineer intern at Facebook, and I've previously interned at Microsoft, ViaSat, and CATT Laboratory.

Terrapin Hackers is a group of over 1,200 students who travel all over North Ameirca to learn and win at hackathons. For most of my time at UMD, I've been organizing weekend trips to hackathons where our student hackers learn how to create mindblowing software and hardware projects.

I organize Bitcamp, which is a 1,000-person hackathon hosted every year at University of Maryland and a place to learn and build innovative tech projects. I directed the travel team and managed various logistics for the transportation of student hackers from all over the continent.
